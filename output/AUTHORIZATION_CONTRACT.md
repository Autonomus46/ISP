# AUTHORIZATION_CONTRACT.md

## 1. AUTHORIZATION DOCTRINE

### 1.1 Purpose

The Authorization Contract governs access to system capabilities, visibility surfaces, operational actions, approval workflows, integration boundaries, and infrastructure functions across the ISP Billing & AAA Infrastructure.

Authorization governs access.

Authorization does not govern truth.

Truth ownership remains governed exclusively by authority contracts.

Authorization determines who may access or invoke authorized capabilities.

Authorization never determines business truth.

Authorization never determines operational truth.

Authorization never determines financial truth.

Authorization never determines enforcement truth.

Authorization exists solely as a governance layer above authority ownership.

---

### 1.2 Foundational Principles

Authority owns truth.

Authorization governs access.

Audit governs accountability.

Events govern propagation.

State machines govern lifecycle.

Authorization cannot override authority ownership.

Authorization cannot bypass lifecycle governance.

Authorization cannot bypass auditability.

Authorization cannot bypass reconstruction requirements.

Authorization must remain deterministic.

Authorization must remain replay-safe.

Authorization decisions must always be reconstructable.

Authorization decisions must always be attributable.

Authorization decisions must always be auditable.

---

Authorization does not govern:
