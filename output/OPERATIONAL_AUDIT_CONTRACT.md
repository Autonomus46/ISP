1. Purpose

Define the role of Operational Audit as the authoritative evidence visibility and forensic reconstruction system for the ISP Billing & AAA Infrastructure.

Operational Audit exists to provide deterministic answers to:

What happened
When it happened
Why it happened
Who authorized it
Who executed it
Which authoritative system generated it
Whether it was valid
Whether it can be reconstructed
Whether it can be proven

Operational Audit does not create truth.

Operational Audit consumes truth generated by authoritative systems.
